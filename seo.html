Key Innovation: The Anti-Detection Strategy

The solution specifically targets low "burstiness" and predictability in AI content via a 3-part validation strategy.

1. Self-Critique Loop

The LLM is prompted to critique its own draft for genericism, repetition, and uniformed structure before final delivery.

Benefit: Guarantees unique structure and natural flow.

2. Dynamic Tone Injection

A user-defined tone (e.g., "Informative but playful") is included in every section prompt, ensuring brand voice adherence.

Benefit: Builds E-E-A-T (Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness).

3. Perplexity Rewrites

Specific prompts force the LLM to introduce complex sentence variations, idioms, and natural conversational fillers typical of human writing.

Benefit: The final output consistently passes 3rd-party AI detection tools.
